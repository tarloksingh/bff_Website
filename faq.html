FAQ

What is bff?

Bff is an app to meet new people online instantly who want to be friends too.

How much does it cost?

Bff is free for your first month and then it is $7.99/month.

Who made bff?

Bff was designed and developed by Tarlok Singh and Jaween Ediriweera.

Why did you create bff?

When I was in New York, it was extremely difficult to make new friends quickly. In a place with 8.5 million people you would think that it would be easy but everyone seemed to already have a circle. I then started to wonder what if there was an app that was soley dedicated to making friends in a real way online.

When was bff launched?

Bff was launched in May 2023.

Is bff safe?

bff is designed to be a safe place to interact with new people. Users use their voice instead of text to communicate so it will be extremely difficult to fake who you are. We are working on achieving what it is like to meet people in the real world. Reporting a user is very easy on bff, just hold down a message and hit report and let us know what happened. Users who violate our safety.

Where is bff available?

bff is currently only available in certain cities. We are working on expanding out to more regions.

How do I add friends?

From the main menu go to contacts. In contacts you can add anyone that is there by sending them a voice invite.

Who can see my profile?

Currently it is only possible for your in app friends to see your profile however anyone can see you in the discover section unless they are blocked.

Is there really no texting and only voice messaging?

Yes, we believe texting is a primitive form of communicating online when it comes to meeting new people. Sending a voice note is exactly how we talk when we meet new people in the real world. Voice notes also make it extremely difficult for users to lie about which gender they are and say grotesque things.

Account

How do I remove a friend?

There are three ways, the easiest is in conversations section. Swipe right on the user you want to remove under your list of messages, a trash icon should appear. A prompt should appear allowing you to remove your friend or just delete the conversation, tap remove.

How do I unblock someone?

Go to your Settings and tap Blocked Users. Then tap the user you would like to unblock and follow the prompts on the screen.

Why do I need to give my phone number and contacts to bff?

Your phone number is used to log into your account and your contacts are used to help you make better connections. Your phone number and contacts are never shared with other users.

Are real names required?

Real names are not required but help when meeting new people and building trust. Please do not add in your last name.

How do I report them?

To report a user in discover, on the bottom left of the screen are three dots, tap that and then it will have a report option and select why you are reporting the user. You can also send an email to help@openupfriends.com with a photo of your ID and the phone number you used to sign up. We will review your report and follow up as soon as possible. This process can take up to 7 days.

How do I sign out?

Go to your settings and tap Sign Out.

How do I delete my account?

Go to your settings and select Delete Account. After 24 hours, your account will not be recoverable and will be deleted. If you'd like to reactivate your account before that happens, just login with your phone number.

What age is bff designed for?

Bff is designed anyone who is looking to meet and make new friends, and users under 17 years old are prohibited and should not use or download bff.
